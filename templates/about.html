Our Story

From a Small Office in Indore to a National Force

EUKI India was founded with one question in mind: why do so many quality Indian brands struggle to scale online while inferior products dominate the shelves? The answer, we discovered, was execution — the right strategy, deployed consistently, at the right time.

Starting with just a handful of Amazon sellers in Indore, we built our methodology through trial, data, and relentless iteration. Today, we manage 500+ brands across 7 platforms, with a team of 80+ specialists covering every facet of digital commerce.

Our tagline isn't just a slogan — digital growth truly does start here.
